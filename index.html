Quiz Competition

Organised By:- Abantika Chandra

Student organization AURORA, under the aegis of Division of Student Welfare, Lovely Professional University, came up with a scintillating quiz competition Quizzora. ‘QUIZZORA’ is an online quiz competition which is going to be conducted on a quiz portal.
